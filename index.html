About

Technical Stuff

In the summer of 2013, Matt took an online web design class at Michigan State University. He liked the class so much that when it was over he became the new teaching assistant where he worked for a year until he took an internship at Gravity Works Design + Development in May 2014.

At Gravity Works, he quickly grew out of his role as an intern and into the role of a part-time front-end web developer while he resumed his classes at MSU. When Matt graduates in May 2015, he plans on moving to New York City to work as a front-end web developer.

Relevant Skills:

  • HTML5 and CSS3
  • Sass and Compass
  • Responsive Web Design
  • Cross-Browser Web Development
  • Drupal Theming and Site Configuration
  • Comfortable with Git and Terminal Commands
  • Working Knowledge of JavaScript and jQuery

Non-Technical Stuff

Matt Liebetrau was born in Southwest Michigan where he lived up until college. When he graduated from high school, Matt moved to East Lansing where he went to school at Michigan State University from 2011 until now.

Aside from web development, Matt enjoys keeping busy with some of his favorite hobbies including watching movies and discussing them on his podcast that he started in October 2014. He likes to stay healthy by exercising every day which sometimes includes playing his favorite sport, soccer. When not playing, he follows Liverpool FC even if it means waking up early on the weekend to catch the games. Matt enjoys playing board games with his friends and likes to play the occasional video game when he has time.
